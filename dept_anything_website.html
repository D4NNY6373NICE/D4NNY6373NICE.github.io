Department of Anything

We're responsible for managing, regulating, and improving anything and everything across the nation.

What we do

The Department of Anything oversees policies, standards, and services related to all matters of national importance. We work to ensure that everything functions effectively and efficiently for the benefit of citizens and communities.

Our key responsibilities include:

  • Setting standards for anything that matters
  • Regulating everything of public concern
  • Providing guidance on all relevant topics
  • Supporting local authorities in managing anything
  • Monitoring the quality of everything we oversee
New policy announcement: We're introducing updated guidelines for managing anything effectively. These will come into effect from next month.

Our priorities

We're focusing on key areas to improve outcomes for everyone:

  • Ensuring universal access to anything essential
  • Improving the quality of everything we regulate
  • Supporting innovation in all relevant sectors
  • Reducing barriers to accessing anything important
