111 online

Check your symptoms

If you're worried about a symptom, answer some questions and we will tell you what to do next.

Depending on your symptoms, we might advise you to:

  • get help from 999 or A&E in an emergency
  • go to an urgent treatment centre
  • contact an evening and weekend (out-of-hours) GP or your own surgery
  • book a callback from a nurse
  • see a pharmacist for help with a minor illness
  • look after yourself at home
Start now

Call 111 instead if you:

  • need help for a child under 5
  • have a number of long-term conditions or complex care needs
  • need to get end-of-life care
Other ways to contact 111 if you have a hearing problem or need help in other languages
